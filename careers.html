Interested in Joining Our Team?

Cortex is bringing a consistent and coherent structure to the metaverse. Our goal at Cortex is to allow anyone to build and achieve the benefits of web3, like controlling your entire digital and online presence, from value to data, to web location and even infrastructure. We are looking for a front-end developer. Great opportunity to build the future of Web3.

We Are Seeking:

Senior level front-end dev with successful production deployments under their belt. Must love getting things right for users in a way that’s intuitive. Project management applications experience a plus. Crypto experience a plus, but this is also a great way to break into crypto. Blockchain experience can be helpful to help with a Solidity contract here or there but not required. Our CTO/Architect will handle the robust blockchain tasks. Position is contract-to-hire to ensure it’s a good fit for everyone.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in CS or equivalent experience required.
  • Must ensure the best practices around software development and delivering high-quality software products into production.
  • Ensures that project/department milestones/goals are met and adheres to approved budgets.
  • 5 years experience in the related area as an individual contributor.
  • 1 to 3 years of supervisory experience is preferred.

Our Stack:

  • NodeJS
  • VueJS
  • Electron
  • GraphQL
  • Solidity

Benefits & Perks:

  • Fast-paced and team-oriented environment where you will be instrumental in the direction of the company.
  • Cortex is a 100% remote company - work from anywhere!
  • Competitive compensation
  • Outsized responsibilities & professional development
  • Token access

Things We Want to See From You:

  • Your Resume/CV
  • Your Github/GitLab profile or open-source projects you contributed to.
  • Other representative examples of your work that are relevant to the position

Submit information above to jobs@crtx.app
