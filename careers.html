Cortex is the ultimate tool for creating, sharing, and collaborating on web3. The Cortex application will be a foundational piece of the first complete Web3 stack, with decentralized data, wiki wallet combined with decentralized domains and cloud infrastructure. Cortex will be the consumer and business-facing gateway of this web3 stack, where crypto is built into all aspects of the web, part of an open metaverse where everyone can create a permanent digital home.

Our goal at Cortex is to allow anyone to build and achieve the benefits of web3: letting users control their entire digital and online presence, from value to data, to web location and even infrastructure. Join a dynamic team reshaping the future of web3.
